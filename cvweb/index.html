About

My name is Link, and during my many adventures through my mother land of Hyrule, I have earned the title of Hero of Time. You may be wondering, what is it that someone with such a job description can do? The answer is simple: everything that is asked of me.

Only eighteen of age, I have mastered both the sword and the bow, and am an expert in no less than five different types of weaponry ranging from regular swords to hammers and axes. However, my weapon of choice is the well known Master Sword. Whatever the reason you may need a hero for, I can guarantee skill and experience.

If all of this doesn't have you convinced yet that I am the hero you need, do check out my skills and references, and consider giving me a call. I work any wage, any time, any timeline. Hyah!
